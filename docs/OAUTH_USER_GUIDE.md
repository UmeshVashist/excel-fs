# User Guide: OAuth Signup with Gmail & GitHub

## What's New?

Your application now supports signing up with **Gmail** or **GitHub**! This makes account creation quick and easy while still allowing you to set your own username and password.

## Two Ways to Sign Up

### Option 1: Quick OAuth Signup (Gmail/GitHub)

1. Go to the **Sign Up** page
2. Click either **Gmail** or **GitHub** button
3. Sign in with your provider account
4. Set your **username** and **password** on the setup page
5. Done! You're ready to use the app

**Benefits**:
- ✅ No need to remember another email
- ✅ One-click authentication
- ✅ Extra security from provider's 2FA

### Option 2: Traditional Email & Password Signup

1. Go to the **Sign Up** page
2. Fill in:
   - Username (your unique identifier)
   - Email address
   - Password (min 6 characters)
3. Click **Create Account**
4. Done! Verify your email and start using the app

**Benefits**:
- ✅ Full control of account
- ✅ No third-party connection
- ✅ Can change password anytime

## Login Options

After you've created an account, you can login with:

### With OAuth Provider
- Click **Gmail** or **GitHub** button on login page
- Instantly logged in with your provider account

### With Email/Username & Password
- Enter your **username** or **email**
- Enter your **password**
- Click **Sign In**

## How the OAuth Signup Works (Step-by-Step)

```
1. YOU CLICK GMAIL/GITHUB
            ↓
2. PROVIDER'S LOGIN PAGE OPENS
   (You sign in with your provider)
            ↓
3. PROVIDER CONFIRMS IT'S REALLY YOU
            ↓
4. YOU'RE SENT BACK TO OUR APP
            ↓
5. YOU SET CUSTOM PASSWORD & USERNAME
   (This is NEW - adds extra security!)
            ↓
6. YOU'RE LOGGED IN AND READY TO GO!
            ↓
7. CAN NOW LOGIN WITH:
   - Gmail/GitHub (quick)
   - OR Username + Password (traditional)
```

## Security Features

✅ **Password Protection**: Even OAuth users set their own password  
✅ **Unique Username**: Can't have duplicate usernames  
✅ **Encrypted Passwords**: Passwords never sent in plain text  
✅ **Provider Security**: Benefit from Gmail/GitHub security  
✅ **Session Security**: Automatic logout after inactivity  

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Why do I need to set a password if I use OAuth?
A: This gives you an additional secure login method. You can login using:
- Gmail/GitHub (for quick access)
- Your username + password (backup method)

### Q: Can I change my username?
A: Contact support to change your username. Usernames are unique identifiers.

### Q: Can I change my password?
A: Yes! After logging in, go to Settings and look for the password change option.

### Q: What if I forget my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page to reset it via email.

### Q: Can I link multiple providers?
A: Not yet, but this feature is coming soon!

### Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes! We use:
- Supabase's enterprise-grade authentication
- HTTPS encryption
- Row-level security on your data
- No passwords stored in plain text

## Step-by-Step: First Time Setup

### Creating an Account with Gmail

1. **Navigate to Sign Up**
   - Go to the sign up page
   
2. **Click Gmail Button**
   - You'll be taken to Google's login page
   
3. **Sign In with Gmail**
   - Enter your Gmail credentials
   - Click "Allow" if prompted for permissions
   
4. **Set Your Credentials**
   - You'll be taken back to our app
   - Enter your desired **username**
   - Create a **strong password**
   - Confirm your password
   
5. **Complete Setup**
   - Click "Complete Setup"
   - You'll be logged in and ready to go!

### Creating an Account with GitHub

Same process as Gmail, but using your GitHub account.

### Creating an Account with Email

1. **Navigate to Sign Up**
   - Go to the sign up page

2. **Fill in the Form**
   - **Username**: Choose a unique username
   - **Email**: Enter your email address
   - **Password**: Create a password (min 6 chars)
   - **Confirm**: Re-enter the password

3. **Create Account**
   - Click "Create Account"
   - Verify your email
   - You're all set!

## Logging In

### For OAuth Accounts
- Click the provider button (Gmail/GitHub)
- You'll be logged in automatically

### For Email Accounts
- Enter your **username** or **email**
- Enter your **password**
- Click "Sign In"

## Need Help?

**Problems with Gmail/GitHub login?**
- Clear your browser cookies
- Try in a different browser
- Check you're using the right provider
- Make sure your provider account is active

**Forgot your password?**
- Click "Forgot Password" on login page
- Follow email instructions to reset

**Username already taken?**
- Choose a different username during setup
- Add numbers or special characters: `user123`, `user_123`

## Tips & Tricks

💡 **Save Your Login Method**: Use the same method each time (Gmail/GitHub)  
💡 **Strong Passwords**: Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols  
💡 **2FA**: Enable 2FA on your Gmail/GitHub for extra security  
💡 **Recovery Email**: Keep your recovery email up to date
